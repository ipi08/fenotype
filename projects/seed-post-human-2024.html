Seed for Post_Human — 2024

Prototype artifact imagining a botanical future after human disappearance. The “seed” is a 3D-printed clay + biomaterial composite containing living moss substrates designed to recolonise damaged surfaces.

The work belongs to the Post_Human Express series, examining planetary repair through non-human agency.
