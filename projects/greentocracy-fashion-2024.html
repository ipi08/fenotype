Greentocracy Fashion — 2024

A speculative fashion capsule in which garments grow, decompose, stain, and evolve. The project uses fungal dyes, food-waste leather, and clay-based structures to question the permanence and politics of clothing.

Presented as part of a larger world-building exercise imagining a “greentocracy” ruled by materials rather than humans.
