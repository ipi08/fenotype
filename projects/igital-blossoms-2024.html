Digital Blossoms — 2024

A series of computational flowers generated through Grasshopper and TouchDesigner. Blossoms emerge from noise fields and simulations of synthetic growth, forming a digital garden of unstable geometries.

Each blossom is produced as a still, a loop, or as a physical laser-cut outcome.
