Altar for the Absent Body — Periplus 2025

Developed during the Periplus international residency, this altar explores absence, memory, and ecological grief. The work combines fired clay, ash-based pastes, and site-specific soil to create an object that holds space for bodies no longer present.

The altar echoes post-human ritual architectures and gestures toward new forms of mourning and regeneration.
