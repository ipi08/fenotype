Re-Existence Echoes — Factory Academy 2024

A hybrid installation merging digital archives with bio-material artifacts. The work reflects on migration, memory, repair, and the embodied act of rebuilding identity through matter.

Includes clay extrusions, sound-responsive interfaces, and a mapped projection of living textures.
