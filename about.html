About

feno.
I'm an artist and multidisciplinary designer from Venezuela exploring sustainable fabrication, biomaterials, and immersive environments. My practice merges digital craftsmanship with organic and waste-derived materials to create interactive spaces that reflect my background and belief in resourceful creativity.
